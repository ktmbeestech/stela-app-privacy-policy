# Privacy Policy

_Last Updated: June 18, 2026_

## Overview

Your privacy is important to us. Stela helps users manage personal and group tasks, reminders, recurring schedules, polls, and group conversations while protecting personal information.

By using this application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

# Information We Collect

When you sign in using Google Authentication, we may collect the following information:

- Name
- Email address
- Profile photo URL

Additionally, the application securely stores:

- Personal and group tasks and descriptions
- Reminder schedules
- Recurring task configurations
- Groups, memberships, invitations, and poll activity
- Group chat messages, replies, mentions, and reactions
- Notification preferences and device notification tokens
- Language, appearance, and other application preferences

---

# How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used solely to provide and improve application functionality, including:

- Managing your tasks and reminders
- Scheduling recurring events
- Supporting groups, assignments, polls, and group conversations
- Sending push notifications for reminders, group activity, and messages
- Routing notification taps and replies to the relevant content
- Synchronizing data across your devices
- Improving user experience and application stability

We do **not** sell or rent your personal information. Information is shared only as needed to operate the service, with the group members you choose to collaborate with, and with the service providers described below.

---

# Group Content and Visibility

Content posted inside a group, including group tasks, polls, votes where identities are visible, chat messages, replies, mentions, and reactions, may be visible to other members of that group.

Anonymous polls hide voter identities from group users, but the service may still process account identifiers internally to enforce one vote per member and prevent duplicate voting.

---

# Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your information.

- All communications are encrypted using secure HTTPS protocols.
- Authentication tokens are securely stored on your device.
- Task details and chat message content are encrypted at rest on our servers.
- Access controls restrict group content to authenticated group members.

Server-side encryption is not end-to-end encryption. Our service must process content to deliver application features such as synchronization, notifications, replies, and moderation.

---

# Push Notifications

This application may send notifications related to tasks, reminders, recurring activities, polls, group invitations, and chat messages.

Notification permissions can be managed or disabled anytime through your device settings.

---

# Microphone and Speech Recognition

If you choose to use voice input, the application requests microphone and speech-recognition permission. Audio is used to convert speech into task text through the speech-recognition service available on your device or operating system.

Stela does not intentionally store raw microphone recordings on its servers. You can deny or revoke microphone and speech-recognition access through device settings.

---

# Account Deletion & Data Removal

You have full control over your data.

At any time, you may permanently delete your account through the application's Settings section. Once deleted:

- All tasks
- Polls and templates created by the account, subject to group ownership and integrity requirements
- Chat messages and group activity associated with the account, subject to legal, security, and shared-group integrity requirements
- Notification history
- Device tokens
- Profile information
- Stored preferences

will be deleted or anonymized from our active systems as applicable. Content shared with other group members may need to be retained in anonymized form to preserve shared conversation and activity history.

These services may process limited information necessary for authentication and notification delivery.
